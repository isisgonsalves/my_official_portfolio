  • I’m an aspiring software engineer specializing in artificial intelligence. I recently graduated from the University of the West Indies with First class honors in my bachelor's degree “Computer Science with Business Management (Special)”.

    My journey began with my first Hello World program in 2018 when I took Computer Science as one of my A-level subjects. My teachers, at the time, made me fall in love with code, and well look at me now, 6 years later, in 2024 wanting to apply for my master's degree in Artificial Intelligence or even Software Engineering.

    I have so many ideas and projects I can't wait to bring to life and I am so excited to continue my computer science journey!

  • I am driven and motivated to contribute positively to this world through my insatiable love for learning. My friends tease me if I utter anything about missing school or studying. Despite the banter, the essential truth is learning is a fundamental aspect of who I am, influencing my goals and directing my path.

    My determination though, goes beyond self-growth; it comes from a genuine desire to have a positive, meaningful influence on other people. In the future, I hope to use my abilities and enthusiasm to develop innovative applications and businesses that improve people's quality of life. Transforming the lives firstly of the people of my country, Trinidad and Tobago.

    More specifically, the biggest impact I aim to make is to completely transform the educational system. Through the development and implementation of my programs and apps, I aim to enhance learning and teaching systems while confronting structural issues and guaranteeing that learning turns into an effective agent of change.
